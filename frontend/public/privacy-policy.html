Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 01, 2026

At Travilio, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and process your personal data when using our travel planning and budget architect services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering for an account, such as your username, email address, password, and saved travel itineraries.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate, maintain, and provide the features and functionality of our services, including saving day-by-day itineraries and bookmarking local guides.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising Cookies

We partner with third-party networks like Google AdSense to serve ads on our site. Google uses cookies (such as the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visit to Travilio and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your stored passwords and personal files from unauthorized access.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: privacy@travilio.com
